ùÑû Audio Bias Detection with Audio Bias Score (ABS).

This tool computes an Audio Bias Score to evaluate gender bias in audio datasets based on acoustic patterns. It uses raw statistical features of male and female speakers to identify hidden imbalances in the dataset ‚Äî before any model is trained.

ùÑû Audio Bias Score Formula

Audio Bias Score = ‚àí0.0125 + (0.0001 √ó Cmale) ‚àí (0.0001 √ó Cfemale) + (0.0005 √ó Vmale) ‚àí (0.0005 √ó Vfemale) + (0.0044 √ó Emale) ‚àí (0.0034 √ó Efemale) ‚àí (0.0004 √ó Amale) ‚àí (0.0004 √ó Afemale) ‚àí (0.0334 √ó Pmale) ‚àí (0.0325 √ó Pfemale) + (0.0002 √ó Pmale2) + (0.0002 √ó Pfemale2) ‚àí (0.0002 √ó Pmale √ó Pfemale)

Score Range:

    +10 = 100% male biased dataset.
    ‚àí10 = 100% female bias dataset.
    0 = gender-neutral dataset.

Clarification: For ease of interpretability, the audio bias score is scaled to a [‚àí10, +10] range using Min-Max scaling, with dataset-specific min and max values for calibration.
